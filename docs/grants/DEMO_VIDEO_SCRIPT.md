## English (Short Version)
Koschei Web3 Bridge is an Alchemy-first monitoring and operations layer for Web3 teams. During the funding sprint, Koschei offers three real SaaS credit/support packages on Shopier: Starter (899 TL / 20,000 credits), Pro (2,299 TL / 70,000 credits), and Studio (4,999 TL / 180,000 credits). These are service/credit/support packages only, not investment products, not securities, not token sales, and they do not promise profit sharing.

Safety-first and no-custody scope: no private keys, no custody, no escrow, no user funds held, and no automatic fund transfers. The product is read-only monitoring and developer tooling, and high-impact actions require human approval.

The product ingests webhook and on-chain events, displays them in a structured `event_logs` dashboard, and adds AI explanations to make technical activity easy to understand.
