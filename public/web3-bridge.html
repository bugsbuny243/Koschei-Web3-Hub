Koschei Web3 Bridge

Production-hardening read-only Web3 event monitoring. The bridge records verified webhook events, explains them for humans, and keeps every fund movement outside Koschei.

  • No private keys
  • No custody
  • No escrow
  • No automatic transfers
  • Read-only monitoring
